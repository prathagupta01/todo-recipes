Khichdi

Description

Khichdi is a wholesome Indian comfort food made with rice, lentils (dal), mild spices, and ghee. It is light, nutritious, and easy to digest—often served with papad, achar (pickle), and curd.

  • Taste Profile: spicy, tangy, masala
  • Serve Suggestion: serve hot for the best experience

Ingredients

  • ½ cup rice
  • ½ cup yellow moong dal (washed & soaked for 15 minutes)
  • 1 medium onion (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (optional, chopped)
  • 1 green chili (slit, optional)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (or oil)
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water (for softer khichdi, add more water)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare Base Heat ghee in a pressure cooker/pot. Add cumin seeds, let them splutter. Add ginger, onion, tomato, green chili → sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  2. Add Rice & Dal Add washed rice & soaked moong dal. Stir well with turmeric & salt.
  3. Cook Pour in 3 cups water, mix well. Pressure cook for 3 whistles (or simmer for 20–25 minutes in a pan).
  4. Finish & Serve Open lid, mash slightly with a spoon. Garnish with coriander leaves & drizzle with ghee. Serve hot with papad, achar, or curd.
