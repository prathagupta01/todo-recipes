Honey Chill Potato

Description

Honey Chilli Potato is a popular Indo-Chinese snack, made with crispy fried potato fingers tossed in a tangy, spicy, and slightly sweet honey-chilli sauce. It’s crunchy, saucy, and addictive!

  • Taste: sweet, tangy, soft
  • Serve Suggestion: serve hot for best experience

Ingredients

  • 3 medium potatoes (cut into thin fingers)
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp maida (all-purpose flour)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • ½ capsicum (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Spring onion (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare Potatoes Wash & cut potatoes into thin fingers. Soak in cold water for 10 mins (removes starch). Drain well. Coat with cornflour, maida, and salt.
  2. Fry Potatoes Heat oil and deep fry coated potatoes on medium flame until golden & crispy. Remove on tissue paper.
  3. Make Sauce Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok/pan. Add garlic, chili, onion, capsicum → sauté on high flame for 2–3 mins. Add ketchup, soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, chili flakes → stir well.
  4. Toss with Honey Add fried potatoes to the sauce, toss on high flame. Drizzle honey and mix until well coated.
  5. Serve Garnish with sesame seeds & chopped spring onion. Serve hot as a snack or starter.
